About National Dr. Seuss Day

National Dr. Seuss Day is celebrated on March 2nd, the birthday of Theodor Geisel, better known as Dr. Seuss. It's a day to honor his legacy of imaginative storytelling and to encourage reading and creativity in children and adults alike.
